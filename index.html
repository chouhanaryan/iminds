About Us

i-Minds Academy offers online + live + interactive coaching of Mathematics from Std 2 to 7. We cover SSC / CBSE / ICSE school maths and eventually prepare students for All India Maths Competitive Exams such as Olympiad, IPM etc. Our teaching methodology involves 5 stages - Learn, Practice, Clarify, Revise, Apply. We focus on conceptual clarity, rigorous practice works and in the process build a strong foundation of Maths at an early age.
